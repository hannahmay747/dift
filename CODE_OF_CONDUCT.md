# Code of Conduct

Dift is an open-source project for data professionals of all backgrounds.

## Our standards

We expect contributors to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome newcomers
- Give helpful feedback
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people
- Assume good intent

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer.
